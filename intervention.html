How We Help


Intervention Services


An intervention provides a temporary break from the cycle of addiction, and is often the first step to freedom. Our experienced and proven interventionists help families confront loved-ones in the throes of addiction by staging an intervention.

Understanding the unique nature of each person’s addiction and family dynamics, interventionists offer recovery council and tailor personalized treatment plans, thereby commencing the addict’s road to recovery.


Identifying the need for an intervention is the crucial first step in the process to recovery.

The "warning signs" of addiction:


  • Borrowing/stealing money
  • Aggressive behavior
  • Isolation/withdrawal
  • Deterioration of physical appearance
  • Lack of energy or motivation
  • Problems at work or school
  • Health issues
  • Emotional irregularities

We offer our services nationwide. We have close partnerships with 20 addiction treatment providers in ten states.


Treatment Options:


  • Short-Term Residential Treatment
  • Long-Term Residential Treatment
  • Intensive Outpatient Treatment (IOP)
  • Sober Living Homes
  • Counseling
