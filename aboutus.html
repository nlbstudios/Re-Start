Re:Start, Inc., a 501(c)3 non-profit organization, is a recovery community offering personalized recovery solutions for people struggling with addiction. We consult families about confronting addiction and managing the recovery process. We provide continual guidance as the family traverses the uncomfortable realities associated with interventions and rehabilitation. Additionally, we provide a unique aftercare program, Re:Fuel Mentoring, designed to assist those new to recovery with the transition from treatment into everyday sober living. A ready-made community of rehabilitation mentors, Re:Fuel Mentors provide safeguards against relapse by helping mentees work through Re:Fuel’s clinically sound curriculum.

Re:Start facilitates interventions for those with both drug and process addictions, connects clients and clients’ families with appropriate treatment providers, and mentors clients through our aftercare program, Re:Fuel.

In an effort to alleviate the physical and emotional toil brought on by addiction, Re:Start journeys with clients and families of clients, navigating them through each phase of the recovery journey. Armed with personal success in recovery, the Re:Start community exists to restore the individual and the family.
