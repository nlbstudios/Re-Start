Our Partnerships

How We Work With Our Partners


We have close relationships with the highest quality facilities treating addiction and mental health. In addition to providing intervention services, we are here to counsel the family and the addict on the level of care appropriate for recovery, and develop a personalized treatment plan. Members of the Re:Start community are always available to answer questions, discuss expectations and provide information about the client’s recovery journey, and their role in this process and how they can help increase the likelihood of success for the client’s recovery. Addiction is a family disease, and we are available to provide coaching for family members of addicts to increase the likelihood of healthy future relationships.


Our solutions include:


  • - Short-term or Long-term Treatment
  • - Intensive Outpatient Treatment (IOP)
  • - Sober Living Homes
  • - Counseling
    •     - For clients post treatment
    •     - For family members of addicts
