Scholarships


    Addiction doesn't discriminate, it affects people of all economic statuses. We are passionate about helping people find freedom from addiction, regardless of their financial situation. Although insurance companies provide some coverage for addiction treatment, other costs - such as fees, transportation, sundry items - often are not covered.

    Help us save a life. Sponsor someone in need of rehabilitation and treatment. Our scholarship program includes a strict vetting policy to ensure that all individuals receiving financial help are truly committed to recovery.

    We have generous donors who make this possible.
